Meet Kadustari

Kadustari blends Afrobeat rhythms with modern electronic vibes, creating a sound that's both nostalgic and futuristic. Influenced by African legends and global street culture, their journey began in Nairobi’s vibrant underground music scene. Today, Kadustari continues to push boundaries with every beat.
